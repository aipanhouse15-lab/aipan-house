# Aipan House

Heritage Aipan Art & Home Décor — bringing the sacred beauty of Kumaon's Aipan art into modern homes.

## About

Aipan House is a premium craft business centered on Kumaoni heritage and Aipan art, a traditional red-and-white art form from the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand, India.
